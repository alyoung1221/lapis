Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide an oppurtunity for people to connect and befriend people online

Why?

There is some bad news and good news. The bad news is that the milinieal generation is pretty lonely. According to a You Gov poll, almost a quater of milineals stated in the survey that they had no friends(according to the methodology people 18 and up were surveyed, there is a possibility that members of gen z were combined with the milineal category for this survey.)

As we are social creatures, humans rely and need one another, and not having any friends is a bad thing. Fortunatley there is good news. What if there is a way that we can make some friends based on our interest, whereever you are, and whenever you want. That is what our goal with this website is for you.

About us


Have you ever had niche hobbies, wanted to make new friends, don't have time for clubs, then friend finder is for you. Friend finder allows people to connect with each other and be able to find and match with new friends with same or similar interests.

Welcome to friendfinder. Our goal is to connect people to meet and make friends based on interests and hobbies.
